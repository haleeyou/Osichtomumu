About the College

About Us

The College of Health Technology, Omumu, is dedicated to training competent and compassionate health professionals to serve communities with integrity and excellence.

Brief History

The Community-Based College of Health Technology, located in Ekiti Local Government Area, Kwara South, was established in response to the pressing need for skilled health workers in rural communities.
Founded through the collaborative efforts of the concerned individuals and key stakeholders, the College was created to strengthen primary healthcare delivery in the region. With a focus on practical, community-oriented training.
The College offers accredited programs in the following programmes:
Community Health.
Public Health.
Health Information Management.
Medical Laboratory Technologist.
Pharmacy Technologist.
Over the years to come, the college will grow into a vital institution, producing qualified professionals who will be serving across health facilities in Kwara South and beyond.
Firmly rooted in the values of service, ethics, and community impact, the College continues to play a key role in bridging the healthcare gap in Ekiti and neighboring areas.

Provost Speech

To be a leading institution in healthcare training, producing skilled and innovative professionals who will contribute to a healthier society.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in producing competent and compassionate health professionals, driving improved healthcare outcomes and advancing the well-being of individuals and communities.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values in health technology, fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and community service, and producing graduates who are equipped to make a positive impact in the healthcare sector.
