Admission Requirements

2025/2026 APPLICATION
The College authority hereby invites suitably qualified candidates who desire to be admitted to any of the programmes run by the college to apply for the 2025/2026.

    Opticianry ND (2 years)

    REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION

  • NECO, WAEC, GCE, OR NABTEB
  • Five (5) 'O' level credits in the following subjects: Mathematics, English, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

    Community Health Extension Work - CHEW (3 years)

    REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION

  • NECO, WAEC, GCE, OR NABTEB
  • Five (5) 'O' level credits in the following subjects: Mathematics, English, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

    Pharmacy Technician PHT (3 years)

    REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION

  • NECO, WAEC, GCE, OR NABTEB
  • Five (5) 'O' level credits in the following subjects: Mathematics, English, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

    Medical Laboratory Technician - MLT (3 years)

    REQUIREMENTS FOR ADMISSION

  • NECO, WAEC, GCE, OR NABTEB
  • Five (5) 'O' level credits in the following subjects: Mathematics, English, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

  • Junior Community Health Extension Workers (JCHEW).
  • Pharmacy Assistant.
  • Laboratory Assistant.
