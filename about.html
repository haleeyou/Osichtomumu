About the College

About Us

Brief History

The Community-Based College of Health Technology, located in Ekiti Local Government Area, Kwara South, was established in response to the pressing need for skilled health workers in rural communities. Founded through the collaborative efforts of concerned individuals and key stakeholders, the College was created to strengthen primary healthcare delivery in the region. With a focus on practical, community-oriented training.

Available Programmes

  • Community Health Workers Extension (6 semesters).
  • Pharmacy Technician (6 semesters).
  • Medical Laboratory Technician (6 semesters).
  • Opticianry (ND) (4 semesters).

School of Health Assistants

  • Junior Community Health Extension Workers (JCHEW) (2 semesters).
  • Pharmacy Assistant (2 semesters).
  • Laboratory Assistant (2 semesters).

Over the years to come, the college will grow into a vital institution, producing qualified professionals who will be serving across health facilities in Kwara South and beyond. Firmly rooted in the values of service, ethics, and community impact, the College continues to play a key role in bridging the healthcare gap in Ekiti and neighboring areas.

Provost Speech

Joint Welcome Address by the Chairman, Governing Council, and Provost of Osi College of Health Technology.

Distinguished Guests, Esteemed Faculty, Staff, and Students.

On behalf of the Governing Council and Management, we warmly welcome you to Osi College of Health Technology.
Our mission is clear: to provide quality health education, produce competent professionals, and strengthen healthcare delivery in Nigeria.

We offer a range of programmes designed to meet national priorities and global best practices:

  • Community Health Extension: Preparing frontline workers for primary healthcare.
  • Pharmacy Technician: Assisting with medication dispensing and management.
  • Medical Laboratory Technician: Training experts in diagnostics for prevention and treatment.
  • Optometrist: Conducts comprehensive eye examination and vision tests
    to assess visual acuity and identify vision problems.

Our programmes are delivered by experienced tutors, supported by modern facilities, and aligned with regulatory standards.
At OSCOHT, we do more than teaching, we prepare students for impactful careers and responsible leadership.
Together, let us continue building a college that educates, elevates, and empowers changemakers.

Welcome to Osi College of Health Technology - a future of purpose, progress, and possibility.

Thank you.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in producing competent and compassionate health professionals, driving improved healthcare outcomes and advancing the well-being of individuals and communities.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values in health technology, fostering a culture of excellence, innovation, and community service, and producing graduates who are equipped to make a positive impact in the healthcare sector.
